Burger.
Ingredients
Burger Patty
    Ground beef
    Salt and pepper
Burger Bun
  • Burger buns (regular or sesame seed)
  • Optionally, butter or oil for toasting.
Toppings
  • Lettuce leaves.
  • Sliced tomatoes.
  • Sliced onions (red or white)
  • Pickles.
  • Cheese slices
Condiments
  • Ketchup.
  • Mustard.
  • Mayonnaise
  • Hot sauce.
Seasonings and Spices:
  • Garlic powder.
  • Onion powder
  • Cayenne pepper (for a spicy kick).
Description

A burger is a classic sandwich comprising a seasoned patty of ground meat, typically beef, nestled between two halves of a soft bun. Toppings like lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and cheese are often added, along with condiments like ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise. Cooked to perfection, a burger delivers a delightful blend of savory flavors and textures, making it a beloved choice for a quick and satisfying meal.
